Request Method: {{request}}

GET method is used to request data from the page, and the contents of the request are shown in the URL with a preceding ? mark. Such as a youtube link like: https://www.youtube.com/watch?Something

Both GET and POST requests have arguments, but POST is used to submit data directly within the body of the request, not being visible in the URL. POST requests, while repeated with a page refresh, prompts "The page that you're looking for used information that you entered. Returning to that page might cause any action you took to be repeated." This does not happen to GET requests. Generally speaking, GET requests can be repeated easily and shared, and are less secure than POST. However in reality both requests are unsecured without HTTPS and should not ever be used for sensitive information.
